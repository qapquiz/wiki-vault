# Mario Zechner

Software developer; author of the blog at **mariozechner.at**. Writes candidly on software
development and, in this wiki's first source from him, on the risks of autonomous coding
agents.

## In this wiki
- Author of *"Thoughts on slowing the fuck down"* (2026-03-25), the source behind
  [[agentic-coding-risks]] and the [[compounding-errors]] mental model. His stance: agents
  are useful for scoped, closed-loop, non-critical work with a human quality gate — and
  dangerous when handed the keys to a production codebase.
