# Anthropic

AI company that builds the **Claude** assistant and the Claude Platform (API for the
Claude [[claude-models|model family]]). Publishes product announcements and research on
its blog, and ships platform features aimed at developers building agents.

## Known for (in this wiki)
- The [[claude-models]] tiered family: Opus (frontier), Sonnet (balanced), Haiku (fast/cheap).
- The **advisor tool** and the [[advisor-strategy]] pattern (2026-04-09), a server-side tool
  for mixing model tiers within a single API request.
- Cited in the wild as having built a C compiler with an "agent swarm" (described as "kind of
  broken") and for handing out free agent-coding access over the 2025 Christmas break — both
  flagged in [[mario-zechner]]'s critique of runaway agentic coding ([[agentic-coding-risks]]).
